Our Story

We started Real Learning to give high school students a direct path into software engineering careers without the years spent in college and the burden of student loans. As the best engineers are being busy doing the job for a very high pay, teaching is often left to "those who can't do".

Moreover, most colleges and online courses tend to only teach basic programming, and then completely veer off to frankly esoteric topics, virtually useless in the real world. But even for the relevant topics the learning process somehow manages to be boring compared to the actual job.

We created Real Learning to change that.

Our program is built and led by software engineers with successful careers at the top companies. We have optimized the curriculum to get to the interesting part where you build projects as soon as possible. At that point we teach both programming and software engineering skills by providing projects of increasing scope and complexity, until they become indistinguishable from the real world projects.
